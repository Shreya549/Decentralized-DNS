ABOUT THE PLATFORM

Our aim of the project is to build a Decentralized Application for a secure Blockchain Based DNS Infrastructure.

Our idea is to offer a free and secure decentralized alternative. It is a blockchain-based DNS software suite that replaces the existing DNS Master-Slave architecture for Root and Name Servers in the DNS and delivers MITM proofs of authentication.

First of all, you need to do Calculate the Reservation Time for which you want to register your domain, based on how much ether to spend and how long is your domian name to register it will calculate the domain register time, Then Log In using your email (sign up if you haven't already) and Password, and then you have access to your dashboard which is directly linked to the Ethereum Ropsten Test Network,

You can reserve Domain Names, attach Domain Addresses to your reserved Domains, visualize your wallet, Extend or Delete Reservation Times, and a lots more. All with the help of this platform.
